Problems Many Guitarists Face

  • They learn dozens of scales as isolated fretboard shapes
  • They get stuck in a few positions on the neck
  • They memorize chord shapes with no connection between them
  • They guess at which notes and chords will sound good together

The Solution: One Universal Reference

  • Master the major scale → spend less time learning other scales
  • At minimum, learning the C major scale across the fretboard teaches you all the natural note names
  • See connections between scales, chords, and progressions
  • Provides a language to understand and communicate music
