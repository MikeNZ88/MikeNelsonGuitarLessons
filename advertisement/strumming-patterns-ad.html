The Golden Rule of Strumming

Your strumming hand should move continuously down-up-down-up like a metronome.
The magic: not every movement needs to hit the strings!

Consistent hand movement = Perfect timing

How This Teaching Tool Works

• Visual Learning: See exactly when to strum and when to use ghost strums
• Audio Feedback: Synthesized chord sounds help you hear the rhythm
• Tempo Control: Speed up or slow down patterns
• Practice Mode: Loop patterns to play along
• Sound Options: Change to percussive sounds to play along
Note: Audio uses synthesized chord tones - practice with your real guitar for best results!
